Based on the graphs and the tables above, we can observe that on average, some positions rank higher overall on one team compared to the other. For example, on Manchester United the average Striker overall is less than the average Manchester City overall. When analyzed for all positions we can note that Manchester United has a higher overall on average for Right Midfielder(RM), Left Wing(LW), Left Back(LB), Goal Keeper(GK), Center Midfielder(CM), Central Defensive Midfielder(CDM), and Center Back(CB).

When looking at what positions have higher overalls on average for Manchester City we see that it is higher for Center Attacking Midfielder(CAM), Right Back(RB), Right Center Back(RCB), Right Wing(RW), and Striker(ST).

Overall from this we can see that of the twelve shared positions between the two teams, Manchester United beats Manchester City on average overall ratings by position of a margin of 7-5.

Conclusion

After comparing the two teams, I would say that they are fairly evenly matched. Both teams are stronger in certain positions than the other but overall they still stay equal. When looking at the average player overall on Manchester United against City, we can see that Manchester United players are rated about 0.515 overall points higher than the average Manchester City player. This tells us that Manchester United is slightly more skilled than Manchester City.

Next, when looking at the breakdown of the player overalls by position, we can see that once again, Manchester United comes on top as the average overall is greater for Manchester United in 7 of the 12 shared positions between the two teams. Manchester City only has 5 positions that have a higher average overall compared to Manchester United.

However, when we look at the totals for the Offense, Midfield, and Defense, we can see that Manchester City has a higher total for Offense and Defense and Manchester United has a higher total for Midfield. Taking a closer look, we can observe that the totals for the two teams are not too far apart. Manchester United’s midfield is roughly 32.833 overall points higher than Manchester City’s midfield. Similarly, Manchester City’s defense is only 10.833 overall points higher than Manchester United’s defense. The offense category is a bit different because Manchester City’s offense rating is about 81.417 overall points higher than Manchester United’s offense. This makes sense because we can see that Manchester City has many higher rated attackers(LW, ST, RW) compared to Manchester United.

Therefore, both teams are evenly matched and are only slightly better than eachother in certain categories.
